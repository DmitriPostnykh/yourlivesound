About Me

More than 25 years ago, I consciously chose the direction of sound engineering. Over the years of my career, working with famous musicians, artists, production centers, theaters, recording studios, I have accumulated a lot of knowledge and experience. I cannot highlight any one direction that interests me most. They are all multifaceted and interesting in their own way. To create high-quality sound, you need to understand and know many important disciplines, such as physics, music theory, harmony and rhythm, mathematics, psychoacoustics, electronics and engineering, modern technologies. Quite a lot, agree, but this is only the tip of the iceberg. In addition to all this, you need to have talent, hearing, patience, stress resistance, the ability to work with people, and a lot of experimenting, comparing, and most importantly, being pleased with the efforts invested!

My goal is simply to create an atmosphere that will not leave any listener indifferent.

Services

Consultations and Equipment Setup

  • Selection of sound equipment suitable for various rooms and open-air venues.
  • Consultation on optimizing equipment performance for stable and high-quality sound.
  • Technical support and recommendations for setting up the sound system for your event.

Concert Work

  • Professional setup of sound equipment for musical performances. FOH System, Monitors System, IEM System, PA System, RF System, Recording System.
  • Support and optimization of sound in real-time at events and shows.
  • Preparation for long trips, tours, and tours. Compiling Rider List, Input-Output List, Stage Plan, calculating sound pressure and loads, ensuring stable sound quality at every stage.

Master Classes for Bands

  • Consultation on the overall sound of the team.
  • Solving various problems at live concerts.
  • Qualified correction at the rehearsal stage.

Studio Work

  • Mixing and mastering of recorded material.
  • Creating noise paintings and sound design for films, TV shows, and television programs.
  • Editing narrators' voices and processing audio for podcasts and audio projects.
  • Writing a custom song, including the entire cycle from creating the music to final production.
